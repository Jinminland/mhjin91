About This Free SVG Converter

Instant SVG Lab is a free online SVG converter that helps you transform PNG, JPG, JPEG, and WEBP images into scalable SVG files. You can upload multiple files at once, preview each SVG result, and download files individually or as a ZIP archive.

Why Use an Online PNG to SVG Converter?

SVG files are scalable vector graphics, which means they stay sharp at any size. They are useful for icons, logos, design assets, simple illustrations, and web graphics. Our batch SVG converter makes it easy to convert multiple image files in one place without installing extra software.
